>[!help] 万能公式
>$\sin\theta=2\sin\dfrac{\theta}{2}\cos\dfrac{\theta}{2}=\dfrac{2\sin\frac{\theta}{2}\cos\frac{\theta}{2}}{\sin^2\frac{\theta}{2}+\cos^2\frac{\theta}{2}}=\dfrac{2\tan\frac{\theta}{2}}{1+\tan^2\frac{\theta}{2}}$
>$\cos\theta=\cos^2\dfrac{\theta}{2}-\sin^2\dfrac{\theta}{2}=\dfrac{\cos^2\frac{\theta}{2}-\sin^\frac{\theta}{2}}{\sin^2\frac{\theta}{2}+\cos^2\frac{\theta}{2}}=\dfrac{1-\tan^2\frac{\theta}{2}}{1+\tan^2\frac{\theta}{2}}$
>$\tan\theta=\dfrac{2\tan\frac{\theta}{2}}{1-\tan^2\frac{\theta}{2}}$

>[!tip] 应用场景
>$y=\dfrac{x}{1+x^2}的值域$
>$设x=\tan\theta,则$
>$y=\dfrac{x}{1+x^2}\Rightarrow y=\dfrac{1}{2}\dfrac{2\tan\theta}{1+\tan^2\theta}=\dfrac{1}{2}\sin2\theta$
>$所以值域为[-\dfrac{1}{2},\dfrac{1}{2}]$
