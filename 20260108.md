>[!success] 综合点的
>$已知正实数x满足(\lg x)^{2025}<(\lg x)^{2024}<(\lg x)^{2026}$

>[!解析]
>$令a=\lg x$
>$a^{2025}<a^{2024}\Rightarrow a^{2025}-a^{2024}=a^{2024}(a-1)<0\Rightarrow a<1,a\neq0$
>$a^{2026}<a^{2024}\Rightarrow a^{2026}-a^{2024}=a^{2024}(a+1)(a-1)<0\Rightarrow a<-1或a>1$
>$\therefore a<-1$
>$即\lg x<-1=\lg 10^{-1}\Rightarrow 0<x<\frac{1}{10}$
