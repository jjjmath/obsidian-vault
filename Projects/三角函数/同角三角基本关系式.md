>[!note]
$\sin^2\alpha+\cos^2\alpha=1$
$\tan\alpha=\dfrac{\sin\alpha}{\cos\alpha}$

> [!SUMMARY]
> $\tan\alpha+\dfrac{1}{\tan\alpha}=\dfrac{1}{\sin\alpha\cos\alpha}$
$\sin^2\alpha=1-\cos^2\alpha$
$\sin^2\alpha=(1-\cos\alpha)(1+\cos\alpha)$
$\dfrac{\sin\alpha}{1+\cos\alpha}=\dfrac{1-\cos\alpha}{\sin\alpha}$
$\dfrac{\sin\alpha}{1+\cos\alpha}=\dfrac{1-\cos\alpha}{\sin\alpha}=\dfrac{1+\sin\alpha-\cos\alpha}{1+\sin\alpha+\cos\alpha}$


> [!example] **题目1**
> 
> $化简\sqrt{\dfrac{1+\sin x}{1-\sin x}},x在第三象限$
