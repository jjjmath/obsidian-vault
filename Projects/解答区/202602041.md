>$已知函数f(x)=\sin(\omega x+\dfrac{\pi}{6})-\cos\omega x(\omega>0)图象的两条相邻$
>$对称轴键的距离为\dfrac{\pi}{4}$
>$(1)求f(x)的解析式$
>$(2)先将f(x)图象上所有点的横坐标扩大为原来的2倍(纵坐标不变)，$

>$求实数m的取值范围和x_1+2x_2+x_3的值$

>[!help] 解析
>$(1)化一$
>$f(x)=\dfrac{\sqrt3}{2}\sin \omega x+\dfrac{1}{2}\cos \omega x-\cos\omega x$
>$=\dfrac{\sqrt3}{2}\sin \omega x-\dfrac{1}{2}\cos \omega x$
>$=\sin(\omega x-\dfrac{\pi}{6})$
>$\because两条相邻对称轴键的距离为\dfrac{\pi}{4}$
>$\therefore 周期T=\dfrac{\pi}{2},\omega=4$
>$\therefore f(x)=\sin(4x-\dfrac{\pi}{6})$
