解析：
$①\frac{\sin\alpha+2\cos\alpha}{\sin\alpha-\cos\alpha}$
$=\frac{\tan\alpha+2}{\tan\alpha-1}=4$
$②\sin\alpha\cos\alpha$
$=\frac{\sin\alpha\cos\alpha}{\sin^2\alpha+\cos^2\alpha}$
$=\frac{\tan\alpha}{\tan^2\alpha+1}$
$=\frac{2}{5}$
