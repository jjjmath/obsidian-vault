>[!tip]  永恒的经典
>引例：
>$f(x)=|\lg (x-1)|，f(a)=f(b)(a\neq b),$
>$\lg(a-1)=-\lg(b-1),(a-1)(b-1)=1$


>[!success] 类似问题
>$已知x_1,x_2是函数f(x)=(\dfrac{1}{2})^x-|\log_2(x-1)|的两个零点$
>$则(x_1-1)(x_2-1)所在的区间是()$
>- [ ] $A.(0,\dfrac{1}{2})$
>- [ ] $B.(\dfrac{1}{2},1)$
>- [ ] $C.(1,2)$
>- [ ] $D.(2,+\infty)$

>[!解析]-
>$研究g(x)=(\dfrac{1}{2})^x与h(x)=|\log_2(x-1)|的交点关系$
>![[l零点经典问题.excalidraw]]
>$设h(m)=h(x_2)$
>$如图(m-1)(x_2-1)=1$
>$x_1<m$
>$\therefore (x_1-1)(x_2-1)<1$
>$(\dfrac{1}{2})^{\dfrac{3}{2}}<1,|\log_2(\dfrac{3}2-1)|=1$
>>$\color{red}f(\dfrac{3}{2})<0,f(1)>0$
>
>$\therefore \dfrac{3}{2}<x_1<2$
>$x_1-1>\dfrac{1}{2},x_2-1>1$
>$(x_1-1)(x_2-1)的值在区间(\dfrac{1}{2},1)$
