>[!success] 3.基底化
>$在平行四边形ABCD中,AB=4,AD=3,点E是AB的中点,点F满足$
>$\overrightarrow{BF}=2\overrightarrow{FC},且DF=\sqrt{13},求\overrightarrow{BF}\cdot\overrightarrow{DF}$

>[解析]
>
>![[202601292tu]]
>$用基底统一向量，由条件选择\overrightarrow{AB},\overrightarrow{AD}$
>$\overrightarrow{DF}=\overrightarrow{DC}+\overrightarrow{CF}=\overrightarrow{AB}-\dfrac{1}{3}\overrightarrow{AD}$
>$\overrightarrow{EF}=\dfrac{1}{2}\overrightarrow{AB}+\dfrac{2}{3}\overrightarrow{AD}$
>$\overrightarrow{DF}{}^2=(\overrightarrow{AB}-\dfrac{1}{3}\overrightarrow{AD}){}^2=\overrightarrow{AB}{}^2-\dfrac{2}{3}\overrightarrow{AB}\cdot\overrightarrow{AD}+\dfrac{1}{9}\overrightarrow{AD}{} ^2=13$
>$\therefore\overrightarrow{AB}\cdot\overrightarrow{AD}=6$

$\overrightarrow{BF}\cdot\overrightarrow{DF}=(\overrightarrow{AB}-\dfrac{1}{3}\overrightarrow{AD})\cdot(\dfrac{1}{2}\overrightarrow{AB}+\dfrac{2}{3}\overrightarrow{AD})=9$
