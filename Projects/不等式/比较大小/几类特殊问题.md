>[!example] 不等式性质
>$已知1<x+y<2,2<x-y<4,求2x+3y的取值范围$

>[!解析]-
>待定系数法
>$2x+3y=m(x+y)+n(x-y)$
>$m=\frac{5}{2},n=-\frac{1}{2}$
>$\frac{5}{2}<\frac{5}{2}(m+n)<5,-2<-\frac{1}{2}(x-y)<-1$
>$\frac{1}{2}<2x+3y<4$
