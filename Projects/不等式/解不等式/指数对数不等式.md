>[!note] 📚**指数不等式**

>[!success] 基本形式
>$(\frac{1}{2})^x>3\Rightarrow (\frac{1}{2})^x>(\frac{1}{2})^{\log_{\frac{1}{2}}3}\Rightarrow x<\log_{\frac{1}{2}}3$



>[!note] 📚**对数不等式**

>[!success] 基本形式
>$\log_{\frac{1}{2}}x>\frac{3}{2}\Rightarrow \log_{\frac{1}{2}}x>\log_{\frac{1}{2}}(\frac{1}{2})^{\frac{3}{2}}\Rightarrow 0<x<(\frac{1}{2})^{\frac{3}{2}}=\frac{\sqrt2}{4}$
