>[!note] 复数的乘法
>$z_1=a+b\text{i},z_2=c+d\text{i},z_1z_2=(ac-bd)(ad+bc)\text{i}$

>[!note] 复数的除法
>$z_1=a+b\text{i},z_2=c+d\text{i},\dfrac{z_1}{z_2}=\dfrac{(a+b\text{i})(c-d\text{i})}{c^2+d^2}=\dfrac{ac+bd+(bc-ad)\text{i}}{c^2+d^2}$

>计算
>$(1)(2+2\text{i})(2-2\text{i})$
>$(2)\dfrac{1-\text{i}}{1+\text{i}}$

>[!tip] 解析
