>[!success] 题目1
>$设0<x<y<\frac{\pi}{2},且\tan y=\tan x+\frac{1}{\cos x},则y-\frac{x}{2}=()$

>[!help] 解析
>$常规思路:切化弦，统一三角函数名$
>$\tan y=\tan x+\frac{1}{\cos x}可化为$
>$\frac{\sin y}{\cos y}=\frac{\sin x+1}{\cos x}$
>$整理可得\sin y\cos x=\cos y\sin x+\cos y$
>$即\sin y\cos x-\cos y\sin x=\cos y\Rightarrow$
>$\sin(y-x)=\cos x=\sin(\frac{\pi}{2}-x)$

>[!help] 判断角的范围（此步骤是常规问题必备思考步骤）
>$\because 0<x<y<\frac{\pi}{2}$
>$\therefore 0<y-x<\frac{\pi}{2}$
>$0<\frac{\pi}{2}-x<\frac{\pi}{2}$
>$\therefore y-x=\frac{\pi}{2}-x$
>$y-2x=\frac{\pi}{2}$
>$结果为\frac{\pi}{4}$

>[!tip] 另解
>$利用半角公式\tan\frac{\theta}{2}=\frac{\sin\frac{\theta}{2}}{\cos\frac{\theta}{2}}=\frac{2\sin^2\frac{\theta}{2}}{2\sin\frac{\theta}{2}\cos\frac{\theta}{2}}=\frac{1-\cos\theta}{\sin\theta}$
>$\tan y=\frac{1+\sin x}{\cos x}=\frac{1-\cos(x+\frac{\pi}{2})}{\sin(x+\frac{\pi}{2})}=\tan(\frac{x}{2}+\frac{\pi}{4})$
>$\because 0<x<y<\frac{\pi}{2}$
>$\therefore 0<\frac{x}{2}+\frac{\pi}{4}<\frac{\pi}{2}$
>$\therefore y=\frac{x}{2}+\frac{\pi}{4}$
