>[!success] 题目1
>$已知f(x)=|\frac{1}{x}-1|+\frac{1}{2}(x>0)$
>$(1)若0<n<m时,f(m)=f(n),求\frac{1}{m}+\frac{1}{n}的值$
>$(2)若0<n<m时,f(x)的定义域与值域均为[n,m],求所有的m、n值$

>[!help] 解析
>$f(m)=f(n)\Rightarrow |\frac{1}{m}-1|+\frac{1}{2}=|\frac{1}{n}-1|+\frac{1}{2}$
>$\frac{1}{m}-1=\frac{1}{n}-1或\frac{1}{m}-1=-\frac{1}{n}+1$
>$\because 0<n<m$
>$\therefore \frac{1}{m}-1=-\frac{1}{n}+1\Rightarrow \frac{1}{m}+\frac{1}{n}=2$

>[!help] (2)
>$f(x)在(0,1]递减，[1,+\infty)递增$
>$1^\circ,0<n<m\le1时，定义域为[n,m],值域为[n,m]$
>$\begin{cases}f(n)=m\\ f(m)=n\end{cases}\Rightarrow\begin{cases}\frac{1}{n}-\frac{1}{2}=m\\ \frac{1}{m}-\frac{1}{2}=n\end{cases}$
>$两式相减得mn=1,不合题意舍去$
>$2^\circ 1\le n<m时$
>$\begin{cases}f(m)=m\\ f(n)=n\end{cases}\Rightarrow\begin{cases}-\frac{1}{m}+\frac{3}{2}=m\\ -\frac{1}{n}+\frac{3}{2}=n\end{cases}无实数解$
>$3^\circ0<n<1<m时$
>$\begin{cases}f(1)=n\\ f(n)=m\end{cases}\Rightarrow\begin{cases}n=\frac{1}{2}\\ m=\frac{3}{2}\end{cases}$
