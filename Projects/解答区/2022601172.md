>$已知函数f(x)=\sin(\omega x+\frac{\pi}{3})(\omega>0)在(\frac{\pi}{6},\frac{\pi}{3})上有最小值，无最大值，且f(\frac{\pi}{6})=f(\frac{\pi}{3})$
>$(1)求f(x)的最小正周期$
>$(2),将函数f(x)的图象向右平移\varphi(0<x<\frac{\pi}{6})个单位长度后等到$

>$(ⅰ)求\varphi的值$
>$(ⅱ)若对任意的x_1,x_2\in[-\frac{\pi}{7},\frac{\pi}{7}],都有f(x_1)<g(x_2)+m成立$
>$求实数m的取值范围$

>[!help] (1)
>$只有最小值没有最大值,且f(\frac{\pi}{6})=f(\frac{\pi}{3})$
>$所以x=\frac{\pi}{4}时，取得最小值$
>$且\frac{\pi}{3}-\frac{\pi}{6}=\frac{\pi}{6}\le T=\frac{2\pi}{\omega}\Rightarrow 0<\omega\le12$
>$f(\frac{\pi}{4})=\sin(\frac{\pi\omega}{4}+\frac{\pi}{3})=-1\Rightarrow \frac{\pi\omega}{4}+\frac{\pi}{3}=-\frac{\pi}{2}+2k\pi,k\in Z$
>$\omega=-\frac{10}{3}+8k$
>$k只能等于1，\omega=\frac{14}{3}$
>$所以f(x)的最小正周期为\frac{3\pi}{7}$
