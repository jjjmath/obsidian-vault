>[!success] 题目
>$已知3\sin\alpha+\sin\beta=\sqrt{10},且\alpha+\beta=\frac{3\pi}{2},求\sin\alpha$

>[!help] 解析
>$\sin\beta=\sin(\frac{3\pi}{2}-\alpha)=-\cos\alpha$
>$\begin{cases}3\sin\alpha-\cos\alpha=\sqrt{10}\cdots①\\\sin^2\alpha+\cos^2\alpha=1\cdots②\end{cases}$
>$由①\cos\alpha=3\sin\alpha-\sqrt{10}代入②$
>$整理得(\sqrt{10}\sin\alpha-3)^2=0$
>$\sin\alpha=\frac{3\sqrt{10}}{10}$
