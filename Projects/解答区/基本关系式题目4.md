**题目4**
$已知\alpha\in(0,\frac{\pi}{4}),\sin\alpha\cos\alpha=\frac{12}{25},则$
- [x] $\sin\alpha+\cos\alpha=\frac{7}{5}$
- [ ] $\sin\alpha-\cos\alpha=\frac{1}{5}$
- [x] $\sin\alpha=\frac{3}{5}$
- [x] $\tan\alpha=\frac{3}{4}$

>[!tip]
>$\alpha\in(0,\frac{\pi}{4}),\sin\alpha<\cos\alpha是关键$
>==思考$\sin\alpha<\cos\alpha,\alpha$的取值范围是什么==

>[!note]
>$\sin^2\alpha+\cos^2\alpha=1的应用$
