>[!question] 题目1
>$如图,G为\triangle ABC的重心，过重心G做直线l与AB,AC分别交于M,N$
>$\overrightarrow{AM}=\lambda\overrightarrow{AB},\overrightarrow{AN}=\mu\overrightarrow{AC},求\dfrac{1}{\lambda}+\dfrac{1}{\mu}$
![[202601287tu|400]]

>[!success] 解析
>$由重心的定义,P为BC的中点，\overrightarrow{AG}=\dfrac{2}{3}\overrightarrow{AP}$
>$\because M,N,G三点共线$
>$\therefore 存在t\in R使得\overrightarrow{AG}=t\overrightarrow{AM}+(1-t)\overrightarrow{AN}$
>$=t\lambda\overrightarrow{AB}+(1-t)\mu\overrightarrow{AC}\cdots①$
>$\overrightarrow{AP}=\dfrac{1}{2}\overrightarrow{AB}+\dfrac{1}{2}\overrightarrow{AC}$
>$\overrightarrow{AG}=\dfrac{1}{3}\overrightarrow{AB}+\dfrac{1}{3}\overrightarrow{AC}\cdots②$
>$由平面向量的基本定理及①②得$
>$\begin{cases}t\lambda=\dfrac{1}{3}\\(1-t)\mu=\dfrac{1}{3}\end{cases}\Rightarrow\begin{cases}3t=\dfrac{1}{\lambda}\\3(1-t)=\dfrac{1}{\mu}\end{cases}\Rightarrow\dfrac{1}{\lambda}+\dfrac{1}{\mu}=3$

>[!question] 练习
>$如图,P为BC的三等分点靠近B，AG=\dfrac{2}{3}AP,过G做直线l与AB,AC$
>$分别交于M,N,且\overrightarrow{AM}=\lambda\overrightarrow{AB},\overrightarrow{AN}=\mu\overrightarrow{AC},求\dfrac{2}{\lambda}+\dfrac{1}{\mu}$
>![[202601288tu|400]]

>[!答案]-
