>[!success] 方程类
>$已知函数f(x)=\begin{cases}-x^2+4x,2\le x\le3\\\frac{x^2+2}{x},3<x\le4\end{cases},g(x)=ax+1,若对任意x_1\in[2,4],$
>$存在x_2\in[-2,1],使得f(x_1)=g(x_2),求实数a的取值范围$

>[!help] 解析
>$由题可知，f(x)的值域为g(x)的值域的子集$
>$2\le x\le3时,f(x)\in[3,4]$
>$3<x\le4时,f(x)\in[\frac{11}{3},\frac{9}{2}]$
>$\therefore f(x)的值域为[3,\frac{9}{2}]$

>[!tip] continue
>$对于g(x)要分类讨论$
>$1^\circ a=0,此时g(x)的值域为\{1\}$
>$[3,\frac{9}{2}]\subseteq\{1\}不成立$
>$2^\circ a>0,g(x)的值域为[-2a+1,a+1]$
>$[3,\frac{9}{2}]\subseteq[-2a+1,a+1]$
>$\therefore \begin{cases}a>0\\-2a+1\le3\\a+1\ge\frac{9}{2}\end{cases},解得a\ge\frac{7}{2}$
>$3^\circ a<0,g(x)的值域为[a+1,-2a+1]$
>$\therefore \begin{cases}a<0\\a+1\le3\\-2a+1\ge\frac{9}{2}\end{cases},解得a\le-\frac{7}{4}$

>[!help] 综上所述
>$a\ge\frac{7}{2}或a\le-\frac{7}{4}$
