>[!help] 万能公式
>$\sin\theta=2\sin\frac{\theta}{2}\cos\frac{\theta}{2}=\frac{2\sin\frac{\theta}{2}\cos\frac{\theta}{2}}{\sin^2\frac{\theta}{2}+\cos^2\frac{\theta}{2}}=\frac{2\tan\frac{\theta}{2}}{1+\tan^2\frac{\theta}{2}}$
>$\cos\theta=\cos^2\frac{\theta}{2}-\sin^2\frac{\theta}{2}=\frac{\cos^2\frac{\theta}{2}-\sin^\frac{\theta}{2}}{\sin^2\frac{\theta}{2}+\cos^2\frac{\theta}{2}}=\frac{1-\tan^2\frac{\theta}{2}}{1+\tan^2\frac{\theta}{2}}$
>$\tan\theta=\frac{2\tan\frac{\theta}{2}}{1-\tan^2\frac{\theta}{2}}$

>[!tip] 应用场景
>$y=\frac{x}{1+x^2}的值域$
>$设x=\tan\theta,则$
>$y=\frac{x}{1+x^2}\Rightarrow y=\frac{1}{2}\frac{2\tan\theta}{1+\tan^2\theta}=\frac{1}{2}\sin2\theta$
>$所以值域为[-\frac{1}{2},\frac{1}{2}]$
