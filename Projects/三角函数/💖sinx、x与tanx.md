>[!note]
>$0<x<\frac{\pi}{2}时,\sin x<x<\tan x$

>[!success]
>如下图：
>$\sin x=AB,\tan x=CD,x=\overset{\frown}{CB}$

![[sinxxtanx.excalidraw]]

>[!tip] 利用几何关系
>$AB<CB$
>$S_{扇形BOC}<S_{\triangle COD}\Rightarrow \frac{1}{2}x\cdot1<\frac{1}{2}CD\cdot1\Rightarrow CB<CD$
