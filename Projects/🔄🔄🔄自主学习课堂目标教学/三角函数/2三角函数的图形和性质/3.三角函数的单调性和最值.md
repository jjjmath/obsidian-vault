>[!danger]- 解答

>$(3)减区间为[2k\pi-\dfrac{\pi}{6},2k\pi+\dfrac{5\pi}{6}],k\in Z;增区间为[2k\pi+\dfrac{5\pi}{6},2k\pi+\dfrac{11\pi}{6}],k\in Z$
>$(4)增区间为[-\dfrac{\pi}{6},\dfrac{5\pi}{6}],k\in Z;减区间为[-\pi,-\dfrac{\pi}{6}],[\dfrac{5\pi}{6},\dfrac{7\pi}{6}],k\in Z$

>[!pro] **题目4**
>$(1)y=\sin(\dfrac{1}{2}x-\dfrac{\pi}{6}),x\in[-\dfrac{2\pi}{3},\dfrac{3\pi}{2}],求值域$
$(2)y=\sin(\omega x)在区间[-\dfrac{\pi}{3},\dfrac{\pi}{2}]上递增，求\omega的取值范围$
$(3)y=\sin(\omega x-\dfrac{\pi}{4})(\omega>0)在区间(\dfrac{\pi}{2},\pi)递增，求\omega的取值范围$

>[!tip]- （1）答案
>$令X=\dfrac{1}{2}x-\dfrac{\pi}{6}\in[-\dfrac{\pi}{2},\dfrac{7\pi}{12}]$
>$考虑y=\sin X,X\in[-\dfrac{\pi}{2},\dfrac{7\pi}{12}]$
>![[三角函数求值域1]]
>$值域为[-1,1]$

>[!tip]- （2）答案
>$由y=\sin\omega x的对称性可知y=\sin\omega x在[-\dfrac{\pi}{2},\dfrac{\pi}{2}]递增，$
>$则T\ge2\pi\Rightarrow \dfrac{2\pi}{\omega}\ge 2\pi$
>$0<\omega\le1$
>$思考为什么\omega一定大于0$

>[!tip]- （3）答案
>$\because x\in(\dfrac{\pi}{2},\pi),\omega>0$
>$\therefore \omega x-\dfrac{\pi}{4}\in(\dfrac{\omega\pi}{2}-\dfrac{\pi}{4},\omega\pi-\dfrac{\pi}{4})$
>根据正弦函数的单调区间则有
>$\begin{cases}\dfrac{\omega\pi}{2}-\dfrac{\pi}{4}\ge-\dfrac{\pi}{2}+2k\pi\\\omega\pi-\dfrac{\pi}{4}\le\dfrac{\pi}{2}+2k\pi\end{cases}k\in Z$
>$解得-\dfrac{1}{2}+4k\le\omega\le\dfrac{3}{4}+2k$
>$只有k=0才能成立$
>$\therefore 0<\omega\le\dfrac{3}{4}$
