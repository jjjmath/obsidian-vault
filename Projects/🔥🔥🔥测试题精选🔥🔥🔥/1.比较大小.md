>[!success] 题目
>$已知函数f(x)=\begin{cases}x^2+e^x,x\ge0,\\x^2+e^{-x},x<0,\end{cases},若a=(\tan\alpha)^{\tan\alpha},b==(\sin\alpha)^{\cos\alpha},c=-(\cos)^{\sin\alpha},\alpha\in(\frac{\pi}{4},\frac{\pi}{2})$
>$则下列结论正确的是()$
>- [ ] $A.f(a)<f(b)<f(c)$
>- [ ] $B.f(b)<f(c)<f(a)$
>- [ ] $C.f(c)<f(b)<f(a)$
>- [ ] $D.f(b)<f(a)<f(c)$

>[!答案]-
>$x>0时,f(x)=x^2+e^x,-x<0,f(-x)=x^2+e^x，f(-x)=f(x)$
>$同理x<0时，f(-x)=f(x),\therefore f(x)为偶函数$
>$x\ge0时，f(x)为增函数$
>$\alpha\in(\frac{\pi}{4},\frac{\pi}{2}),0<\cos\alpha<\sin\alpha<1<\tan\alpha$
>$则0<(\cos\alpha)^{\sin\alpha}<(\cos\alpha)^{\cos\alpha}<(\sin\alpha)^{\cos\alpha}<1,\tan\alpha>1$
>$\therefore a>b>-c>0\Rightarrow f(a>f(b)>f(-c)=f(c)$
>$答案：C$
