>[!success] **题目1**
>
>$已知\triangle ABC中，\sin A=\dfrac{3}{5},\cos B=\dfrac{5}{13},求\cos C$

>[!answer]-
>$在\triangle ABC中，A+B+C=\pi$
>$\cos C=-\cos(A+B)$
>$=-\cos A\cos B+\sin A\sin B$
>$A\in(0,\pi)$
>$\cos B>0,B\in(0,\frac{\pi}{2})$
>$\sin B=\frac{12}{13}$
>$\cos A=\sqrt{1-\sin^2A}=\pm\frac{4}{5}$
>$\because \sin B>\sin A$
>$\therefore A<B$
>$\therefore \cos A>0$
>$\cos A=\frac{4}{5}$
>$\cos C=\frac{16}{65}$

>[!success] 题目2(经典)
>$\sin40^\circ(\tan10^\circ-\sqrt3)$

>[!answer]- 解析
>$原式=\sin40^\circ(\frac{\sin10^\circ}{\cos10^\circ}-\sqrt3)$
>$=\sin40^\circ\frac{\sin10^\circ-\sqrt3\cos10^\circ}{\cos10^\circ}$
>$=\sin40^\circ\frac{2(\frac{1}{2}\sin10^\circ-\frac{\sqrt3}{2}\cos10^\circ)}{\cos10^\circ}$
>$=\sin40^\circ\frac{2\sin(10^\circ-60^\circ)}{\cos10^\circ}$
>$=\frac{-2\sin40^\circ\sin50^\circ}{\cos10^\circ}$
>$=\frac{-2\sin40^\circ\cos40^\circ}{\cos10^\circ}$
>$=-\frac{\sin80^\circ}{\cos10^\circ}=-1$

>[!answer]- 另解
>$原式=\sin40^\circ(\tan10^\circ-\tan60^\circ)$
>$=\sin40^\circ\tan(10^\circ-60^\circ)(1+\tan10^\circ\tan60^\circ)$
>$=-\cos50^\circ\tan50^\circ\frac{\cos10^\circ\cos60^\circ+\sin10^\circ\sin50^\circ}{\cos10^\circ\cos60^\circ}$
>$=-\sin50^\circ\frac{\cos50^\circ}{\cos10^\circ\cos60^\circ}$
>$=-\frac{2\sin50^\circ\cos50^\circ}{\cos10^\circ}=-\frac{\sin100\circ}{\cos10^\circ}=-\frac{\cos10^\circ}{\cos10^\circ}=-1$
