>[!example] **题目1**
>
>化简
>$(1)\cos36^\circ\cos72^\circ$
>$(2)\sqrt{1+\sin4}+\sqrt{1-\sin4}$

>[!answer]- 解答
>$(1)原式=\dfrac{2\sin36^\circ\cos36^\circ\cos72^\circ}{2\sin36^\circ}$
>$=\dfrac{2\sin72^\circ\cos72^\circ}{4\sin36^\circ}=\dfrac{\sin144^\circ}{4\sin36^\circ}$
>$=\dfrac{\sin36^\circ}{4\sin36^\circ}=\dfrac{1}{4}$
>和初中所学有没有相似
>$(2)原式=|\sin2+\cos2|+|\sin2-\cos2|$
>$\because \dfrac{\pi}{2}<2<\dfrac{3\pi}{4}$
>$\therefore \sqrt{1+\sin4}+\sqrt{1-\sin4}=2\sin2$
>==认真思考符号问题==

>[!tip]
>问题(1)可以拓展，你能想到什么？


>[!example] **题目2**
>
>$化简\sqrt{\dfrac{1}{2}-\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{\dfrac{1}{2}+\dfrac{1}{2}\cos2\theta}},\theta\in(\dfrac{3\pi}{2},2\pi)$

>[!answer]- 答案
>$\sin\dfrac{\theta}{2}$

>[!example] **题目3**
>
>$已知\sin(\theta+\dfrac{\pi}{4})=\dfrac{2}{3},求\sin2\theta$

>[!answer]- 答案
>$\sin2\theta=-\cos(2\theta+\dfrac{\pi}{2})=-\cos2(\theta+\dfrac{\pi}{4})$
>$=-[1-2\sin^2(\theta+\dfrac{\pi}{4})]=-\dfrac{1}{9}$
>有几个角度？

>[!example] **题目4**
>
>$已知\sin(\dfrac{\pi}{3}-\theta)=\dfrac{\sqrt2}{3},求\cos(\dfrac{\pi}{3}+2\theta)$

>[!answer]- 答案
>$-\dfrac{5}{9}$

>[!tip]
>寻找题目3、4的相同点

>[!example] **题目5**
>
>$已知\tan\theta=\dfrac{\cos\theta}{4-\sin\theta},求\cos2\theta$


>[!answer]- 答案
>$\dfrac{7}{8}$
