>[!success] 题目
>$已知函数f(x)=e^x-\frac{1}{x}的零点为x_0,求证:e^{x_0}-\ln x_0>2$

>[!help] 解析
>$x<0时,e^x>0,\frac{1}{x}<0,f(x)恒大于0，无零点$
>$x>0时，f(x)=e^x-\frac{1}{x}为增函数$
>$f(\frac{1}{2})=\sqrt e-2<0,f(1)=e-1>0$
>$\therefore x_0\in (\frac{1}{2},1),e^{x_0}=\frac{1}{x_0},且x_0=-\ln x_0$
>$e^{x_0}-\ln x_0=\frac{1}{x_0}+x_0\ge2且等号不能成立$
>$\therefore e^{x_0}-\ln x_0>2$
