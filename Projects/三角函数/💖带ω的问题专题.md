[[4.💖三角函数图像性质综合应用]]

>[!success] 题目1
>$已知函数f(x)=\sin(\omega x+\varphi)(\omega>0,0<\varphi<\pi),在[-\dfrac{\pi}{3},\dfrac{\pi}{6}]上单调，$
>$f(x)的图像关于点(-\dfrac{2\pi}{3},0)中心对称且关于直线x=\dfrac{5\pi}{6}对称，则$
>$\omega的取值个数为（）$

>[!解析] 
>相对于单调性，对称性更方便处理
>$根据对称性得$
>$\begin{cases}-\dfrac{2\pi}{3}\omega+\varphi=k_1\pi...①\\\dfrac{5\pi}{6}\omega+\varphi=k_2\pi+\dfrac{\pi}{2}...②\end{cases},k_1,k_2\in Z$
>$\omega=\dfrac{2}{3}[k_2-k_1+\dfrac{1}{2}]$
>$\omega=\dfrac{2}{3}k+\dfrac{1}{3}(k\in Z)$

---
>[!danger] 下面的常规写法在本题中有些笨拙
>$f(x)=\sin(\omega x+\varphi)(\omega>0,0<\varphi<\pi),在[-\dfrac{\pi}{3},\dfrac{\pi}{6}]上单调$
>$[-\dfrac{\pi}{3}\omega+\varphi,\dfrac{\pi}{6}\omega+\varphi]\subseteq[-\dfrac{\pi}{2}+k_3\pi,\dfrac{\pi}{2}+k_3\pi],k_3\in Z$
>$\begin{cases}-\dfrac{\pi}{3}\omega+\varphi\ge-\dfrac{\pi}{2}+k_3\pi\\\dfrac{\pi}{6}\omega+\varphi\le\dfrac{\pi}{2}+k_3\pi\end{cases}$
>$\varphi 可以用\omega 代换但是很麻烦$
>。。。

---
>[!continue] 换个思路
>$根据单调区间的长度一定小于等于半个周期\dfrac{T}{2}=\dfrac{\pi}{\omega}$
>$[\dfrac{\pi}{6}-(-\dfrac{\pi}{3})]\le\dfrac{\pi}{\omega}$
>$0<\omega\le2$
>$结合\omega=\dfrac{2}{3}k+\dfrac{1}{3}(k\in Z)$
>$k=0,1或2$

>[!continue]
>$k=0时,\omega=\dfrac{1}{3},结合①和0<\varphi<\pi\Rightarrow\varphi=\dfrac{2\pi}{9}$
>$验证f(x)=\sin(\dfrac{1}{3}x+\dfrac{2\pi}{9})在[-\dfrac{\pi}{3},\dfrac{\pi}{6}]上是否单调$

>[!help] 两种方式（1）
>$小题用五点作图观察对称轴或直接找最值T=6\pi$
>$令\dfrac{1}{3}x+\dfrac{2\pi}{9}=\dfrac{\pi}{2}\Rightarrow x=\dfrac{5\pi}{18}$
>$\color{red}最大值左边半个周期为增区间，右边半个周期为减区间$
>$[-\dfrac{\pi}{3},\dfrac{\pi}{6}]\subseteq[-\dfrac{49\pi}{18},\dfrac{5\pi}{18}]$
>$\therefore \omega=0成立$

>[!help] 两种方式（2）
>$直接求f(x)=\sin(\dfrac{1}{3}x+\dfrac{2\pi}{9})的单调区间或$
>$对\dfrac{1}{3}x+\dfrac{2\pi}{9}整体范围进行判断（复合函数的思想）$
>$x\in[-\dfrac{\pi}{3},\dfrac{\pi}{6}]\Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{3}x+\dfrac{2\pi}{9}\in[\dfrac{\pi}{9}\dfrac{5\pi}{18}]\subseteq[-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\dfrac{\pi}{2}]$
>$\therefore \omega=0成立$

>[!continue]
>$对于\omega=1,2同理判断即可$
>$结论：\omega=\dfrac{1}{3}或\dfrac{5}{3}两个$
>
