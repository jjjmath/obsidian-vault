>[!note] 复数的乘法
>$z_1=a+b\text{i},z_2=c+d\text{i},z_1z_2=(ac-bd)(ad+bc)\text{i}$

>[!note] 复数的除法
>$z_1=a+b\text{i},z_2=c+d\text{i},\dfrac{z_1}{z_2}=\dfrac{(a+b\text{i})(c-d\text{i})}{c^2+d^2}=\dfrac{ac+bd+(bc-ad)\text{i}}{c^2+d^2}$

>[!question] 题目1
>计算
>$(1)(2+2\text{i})(2-2\text{i})$
>$(2)\dfrac{1-\text{i}}{1+\text{i}}$

>[!tip] 解析
>$(1)8$
>$(2)-\text{i}$

>[!question] 题目2
>$(1)\dfrac{(1+\text{i})^7}{1-\text{i}}+\dfrac{(1+\text{i})^7}{1+\text{i}}$
>$(2)(-\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}-\dfrac{1}{2}\text{i})^{12}$

>[!tip] 解析
>$(1)$
>$(1+\text{i})^2=2\text{i}$
>$(1+\text{i})^7=(1+\text{i})[(1+\text{i})^2]^3=(1+\text{i})(2\text{i})^3=-8\text{i}(1+\text{i})=8(1-\text{i})$
>所以$)\dfrac{(1+\text{i})^7}{1-\text{i}}=8$
>$\dfrac{(1+\text{i})^7}{1+\text{i}}=\dfrac{8(1-\text{i})}{1+\text{i}}=-8\text{i}$
>所以，原式=$8-8\text{i}$
>算法2
>$\dfrac{(1+\text{i})^7}{1-\text{i}}+\dfrac{(1+\text{i})^7}{1+\text{i}}=(1+\text{i})^7(\dfrac{1}{1-\text{i}}+\dfrac{1}{1+\text{i}})=(8-8\text{i})(\dfrac{1+\text{i}+1-\text{i}}{2})=8-8\text{i}$
>$(2)$
>$(-\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}-\dfrac{1}{2}\text{i})^{2}=\dfrac{1}{2}+\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\text{i}$
>$(-\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}-\dfrac{1}{2}\text{i})^{3}=(\dfrac{1}{2}+\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\text{i})(-\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}-\dfrac{1}{2}\text{i})=-\text{i}$
>所以，原式$=(-\text{i})^4=1$

>[!question] 题目3
>设$\text{i}$是虚数单位，且$\omega=-\dfrac{1}{2}+\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\text{i}$,求$\omega^2+\omega+1$

>[!tip] 解析
>$\omega^3=1,\omega^2+\omega+1=\dfrac{\omega^3-1}{\omega-1}=0$

>[!question] 题目4
>若复数$z$满足$z(1+\text{i})=2\text{i}$，求$|z|$

>[!tip] 解析
>$|z|=|\dfrac{2\text{i}}{1+\text{i}}|=\dfrac{|2\text{i}|}{|1+\text{i}|}=\sqrt{2}$

>[!question] 题目5
>已知复数$z=2-\text{i}$，求$z\cdot\overline{z}$

>[!tip] 解析
>$z\cdot\overline{z}=|z|^2=5$

>[!question] 题目6
>在复数范围内解方程
>$(1)x^2+3=0$
>$(2)2x^2-x+3=0$

>[!tip] 解析
>$(1)x=\pm\sqrt{3}\text{i}$
>$(2)x=\dfrac{1\pm\sqrt{23}\text{i}}{4}$

>[!note] 总结
>$|z_2\cdot z_2|=|z_2||z_2|$
>$|\dfrac{z_1}{z_2}|=\dfrac{|z_1|}{|z_2|}$
>$z\cdot\overline{z}=|z|^2$
