>[!success] 恒等变换
>$已知函数f(x)=2\sqrt3\sin x\sin(x-\frac{\pi}{2})-2\cos^2x+1$
>$(1)讨论函数f(x)在[\frac{\pi}{4},\frac{5\pi}{6}]上的单调性$
>$(2)若f(x_0)=-\frac{6}{5},x_0\in[\frac{\pi}{4},\frac{\pi}{2}],求\cos2x_0的值$

>[!解析]
>$f(x)=2\sqrt3\sin x(-\cos x)-\cos2x$
>$\color{blue}诱导公式和二倍角余弦公式$
>$f(x)=-\sqrt3\sin2x-\cos2x$
>$\color{blue}二倍角正弦公式$
>$f(x)=-2(\frac{\sqrt3}{2}\sin x+\frac{1}{2}\cos x)$
>$f(x)=-2\sin(x+\frac{\pi}{6})$
>$\color{blue}辅助角公式（化一）$

>[!continue]
>$整体思想$
>$(1)\because x\in[\frac{\pi}{4},\frac{5\pi}{6}]$
>$\therefore 2x+\frac{\pi}{6}\in[\frac{2\pi}{3},\frac{11\pi}{6}]$
>$当\frac{2\pi}{3}\le x\le\frac{3\pi}{2},即\frac{\pi}{4}\le x\le\frac{2\pi}{3}时，y=\sin(2x+\frac{\pi}{6})递减$
>$f(x)=-2\sin(2x+\frac{\pi}{6})递增$
>$当\frac{3\pi}{2}\le x\le\frac{11\pi}{6},即\frac{2\pi}{3}\le x\le\frac{5\pi}{6}时，y=\sin(2x+\frac{\pi}{6})递增$
>$f(x)=-2\sin(2x+\frac{\pi}{6})递减$
>$\therefore f(x)在[\frac{\pi}{4},\frac{2\pi}{3}]上递增，在[\frac{2\pi}{3}，\frac{5\pi}{6}]上递减$

>[!tip] 另一种写法
>$-\frac{\pi}{2}+2k\pi\le2x+\frac{\pi}{6}\le\frac{\pi}{2}+2k\pi,k\in Z$
>$解的-\frac{\pi}{3}+k\pi\le x\le\frac{\pi}{6}+k\pi$
>$f(x)的减区间为\cdots[-\frac{\pi}{3},\frac{\pi}{6}],[\frac{2\pi}{3},\frac{7\pi}{6}],[\frac{5\pi}{3}\frac{13\pi}{6}]\cdots$
>$f(x)的增区间为\cdots[\frac{\pi}{6},\frac{2\pi}{3}],[\frac{7\pi}{6},\frac{5\pi}{3}],[\frac{13\pi}{6}\frac{8\pi}{3}]\cdots$
>$\because x\in[\frac{\pi}{4},\frac{5\pi}{6}]$
>$\therefore f(x)在[\frac{\pi}{4},\frac{2\pi}{3}]上递增，在[\frac{2\pi}{3}，\frac{5\pi}{6}]上递减$

>[!continue]
>整体思想
>$(2)由(1)可知,f(x_0)=-2\sin(2x_0+\frac{\pi}{6})=-\frac{6}{5}$
>$\color{blue}不要急于展开，先观察已知角和所求角的关系$
>$即\sin(2x_0+\frac{\pi}{6})=\frac{3}{5}$
>$\because x_0\in[\frac{\pi}{4},\frac{\pi}{2}]$
>$\therefore 2x_0+\frac{\pi}{6}\in[\frac{2\pi}{3},\frac{7\pi}{6}]$
>$\therefore \cos(2x_0+\frac{\pi}{6})=-\frac{4}{5}$
>$\cos2x_0=\cos[(2x_0+\frac{\pi}{6})-\frac{\pi}{6}]$
>$=\cos(2x_0+\frac{\pi}{6})\cos\frac{\pi}{6}+\sin(2x_0+\frac{\pi}{6})\sin\frac{\pi}{6}=\frac{3-4\sqrt3}{10}$
>$\color{blue}虽然是二倍，但用的确不是二倍角公式，$
>$\color{blue}角度关系在解决问题是是要优先考虑的因素$
