$已知函数f(x)=|\lg(x-1)|,若f(a)=f(b)(a<b),M=\frac{a}{a-1}+\frac{2}{b-1},求M的最值$
解析：
$由题可知(a-1)(b-1)=1$
$M=1+\frac{1}{a-1}+\frac{2}{b-1}=1+\frac{1}{a-1}+2(a-1)$
$设a-1=t\in(0,1)$
$M=1+2t+\frac{1}{t}\ge1+2\sqrt2$
$当且仅当，t=\frac{\sqrt2}{2},即x=1+\frac{\sqrt2}{2},等号成立$
