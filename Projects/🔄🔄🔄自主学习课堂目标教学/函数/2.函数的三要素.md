>[!question] - [ ] 任务一
>$\color{blue}求函数解析式$


>[!tip] 题目1
>$已知f(x+1)=x^2+2x,求f(x)$
>>[!答案]-
>>$配凑法$
>>$f(x+1)=(x+1)^2-1,所以f(x)=x^2-1$ 

>[!练习]
>$f(x+\frac{1}{x})=x^2+\frac{1}{x^2}$
>>[!答案]-
>>$f(x+\frac{1}{x})=(x+\frac{1}{x})^2-2$
>>$\therefore f(x)=x^2-2$

>[!success] 题目2
>$已知f(\sqrt x+1)=x^2+2x,求f(x)$
>>[!答案]-
>>$换元法$
>>$令t=\sqrt x+1,则x=(t-1)^2,所以f(t)=(t-1)^4+2(t-1)^2$
>>$即f(x)=(x-1)^4+2(x-1)^2$

>[!success] 题目3
>$已知f(x)+2f(-x)=\frac{1}{x},求f(x)$
>>[!答案]-
>>$用-x代换x$
>>$\begin{cases}f(x)+2f(-x)=\frac{1}{x}\\f(-x)+2f(x)=-\frac{1}{x}\end{cases}$
>>$解得f(x)=-\frac{1}{x}$

>[!success] 题目4
>$已知f(x)的定义域为[-1,1],求f(2x-1)的定义域$
>>[!答案]-
>>$-1\le 2x-1\le 1$
>>$f(x)的定义域为[0,1]$


>[!success] 题目5

>>[!答案]-
