>[!danger] 📚幂型函数+幂型函数

| $f(x)$        | $g(x)$            | $f[g(x)]$                | $g[f(x)]$                       |
| ------------- | ----------------- | ------------------------ | ------------------------------- |
| $\sqrt x$     | $x^2-3x-2$        | $\sqrt{x^2-3x-2}$        | $x-3\sqrt x-2$                  |
| $\sqrt x$     | $\dfrac{x-1}{x+1}$ | $\sqrt{\dfrac{x-1}{x+1}}$ | $\dfrac{\sqrt x-1}{\sqrt x+1}$   |
| $\dfrac{1}{x}$ | $x^2-3x-2$        | $\dfrac{1}{x^2-3x-2}$     | $(\dfrac{1}{x})^2-\dfrac{3}{x}-2$ |

>[!danger] 📚幂型函数+指数型函数

| $f(x)$            | $g(x)$ | $f[g(x)]$             | $g[f(x)]$             |
| ----------------- | ------ | --------------------- | --------------------- |
| $\sqrt x-1$       | $2^x$  | $\sqrt{2^x-1}$        | $2^{\sqrt x}-1$       |
| $x^2-3x-1$        | $2^x$  | $4^x-3\cdot 2^x-1$    | $2^{x^2-3x-1}$        |
| $\dfrac{1-x}{1+x}$ | $2^x$  | $\dfrac{1-2^x}{1+2^x}$ | $2^{\dfrac{1-x}{1+x}}$ |

>[!danger] 📚幂型函数+对型数函数

| $f(x)$            | $g(x)$  | $f[g(x)]$                  | $g[f(x)]$              |
| ----------------- | ------- | -------------------------- | ---------------------- |
| $\sqrt x-1$       | $\lg x$ | $\sqrt{\lg x-1}$           | $\lg \sqrt x-1$        |
| $x^2-3x-1$        | $\lg x$ | $(\lg x)^2-3\cdot \lg x-1$ | $\lg {(x^2-3x-1)}$     |
| $\dfrac{1-x}{1+x}$ | $\lg x$ | $\dfrac{1-\lg x}{1+\lg x}$  | $\lg{\dfrac{1-x}{1+x}}$ |


>[!danger] 📚指数函数+对数函数

| $f(x)$      | $g(x)$ | $f[g(x)]$          | $g[f(x)]$       |
| ----------- | ------ | ------------------ | --------------- |
| $\sqrt x-1$ | $2^x$  | $\sqrt{2^x-1}$     | $2^{\sqrt x}-1$ |
| $x^2-3x-1$  | $2^x$  | $4^x-3\cdot 2^x-1$ | $2^{x^2-3x-1}$  |

>[!danger] 📚对勾函数+

| --------------- | ------- | ----------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| $x+\dfrac{1}{x}$ | $2^x$   | $2^x+\dfrac{1}{2^x}$     | $2^{x+\dfrac{1}{x}}$                 |

| $x+\dfrac{1}{x}$ | $\lg x$ | $\lg x+\dfrac{1}{\lg x}$ | $\lg (x+\dfrac{1}{x})$               |
