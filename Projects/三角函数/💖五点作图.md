>[!success] 题目1
>$已知函数f(x)=\sin(2x-\frac{\pi}{6})$
>$(1)用\text{“五点法”}作出[0,\pi]的图像$
>$(2)求f(x)的对称轴和对称轴中心$
>$(3)解不等式f(x)\ge\frac{\sqrt3}{2}$

>[!解析]
>|$2x-\frac{\pi}{6}$|$0$|$\frac{\pi}{2}$|$\pi$|$\frac{3\pi}{2}$|$2\pi$|
>|---|---|---|---|---|---|
>|$x$|$\frac{\pi}{12}$|$\frac{4\pi}{12}$|$\frac{7\pi}{12}$|$\frac{10\pi}{12}$|$\frac{13\pi}{12}$|
>|$f(x)$|$0$|$1$|$0$|$-1$|$0$|

>[!补两个端点]
$(0,-\frac{1}{2}),(\pi,-\frac{1}{2})$
