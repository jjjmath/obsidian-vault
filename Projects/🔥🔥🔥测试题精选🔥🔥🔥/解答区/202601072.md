>[!success] 二倍角余弦公式
>$若\tan(\alpha +\dfrac{\pi}{6})=\dfrac{\cos(\alpha+\frac{\pi}{6})}{3-\sin(\alpha+\dfrac{\pi}{6})},则\sin(2\alpha-\dfrac{\pi}{6})=()$

>[!解析]
>$\color{orange}切化弦$
>$\dfrac{\sin(\alpha +\dfrac{\pi}{6})}{\cos(\alpha +\dfrac{\pi}{6})}=\dfrac{\cos(\alpha+\dfrac{\pi}{6})}{3-\sin(\alpha+\dfrac{\pi}{6})}$

>[!continue]
>$\color{orange}等价转化$
>${\cos^2(\alpha +\frac{\pi}{6})}={\sin(\alpha+\frac{\pi}{6})}({3-\sin(\alpha+\frac{\pi}{6})})$
>${\cos^2(\alpha +\frac{\pi}{6})}=3{\sin(\alpha+\frac{\pi}{6})}-\sin^2(\alpha+\frac{\pi}{6})$
>${\cos^2(\alpha +\frac{\pi}{6})}+\sin^2(\alpha+\frac{\pi}{6})=3{\sin(\alpha+\frac{\pi}{6})}$
>$\color{orange}同角三角基本关系式$
>$3{\sin(\alpha+\frac{\pi}{6})}=1$
>${\sin(\alpha+\frac{\pi}{6})}=\frac{1}{3}$

>[!continue]
>$\color{orange}以\alpha+\frac{\pi}{6}为基本单位统一角$
>$\sin(2\alpha-\frac{\pi}{6})=\sin(2\alpha+\frac{\pi}{3}-\frac{\pi}{2})$
>$\color{orange}诱导公式$
>$\sin(2\alpha-\frac{\pi}{6})=-\cos(2\alpha+\frac{\pi}{3})=-\cos2(\alpha+\frac{\pi}{6})$
>$\color{orange}二倍角余弦公式$
>$\cos2(\alpha+\frac{\pi}{6})=1-2\sin^2(\alpha+\frac{\pi}{6})=1-2\times\frac{1}{9}=\frac{7}{9}$
>$\therefore 原式=-\frac{7}{9}$
