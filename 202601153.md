>[!success] 题目1
>$已知f(x)=|\frac{1}{x}-1|+\frac{1}{2}(x>0)$
>$(1)若0<n<m时,f(m)=f(n),求\frac{1}{m}+\frac{1}{n}的值$
>$(2)若0<n<m时,f(x)的定义域与值域均为[n,m],求所有的m、n值$

>[!help] 解析
>$f(m)=f(n)\Rightarrow |\frac{1}{m}-1|+\frac{1}{2}=|\frac{1}{n}-1|+\frac{1}{2}$
>$\frac{1}{m}-1=\frac{1}{n}-1或\frac{1}{m}-1=-\frac{1}{n}+1$
>$\because 0<n<m$
>$\therefore \frac{1}{m}-1=-\frac{1}{n}+1\Rightarrow \frac{1}{m}+\frac{1}{n}=2$
