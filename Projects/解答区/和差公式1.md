$已知\sin\alpha=-\frac{3}{5},\alpha为第四象限的角，求\sin(\frac{\pi}{4}-\alpha),\cos(\frac{\pi}{4}+\alpha)$

解析：
>[!step]
$\because\alpha是第四象限的角，\therefore\cos\alpha=\frac{4}{5}$
$\sin(\frac{\pi}{4}-\alpha)=\sin\frac{\pi}{4}\cos\alpha-\cos\frac{\pi}{4}\sin\alpha$
$=\frac{\sqrt2}{2}\times\frac{4}{5}-\frac{\sqrt2}{2}\times(-\frac{3}{5})=\frac{7\sqrt2}{10}$
$\cos(\frac{\pi}{4}+\alpha)=\cos\frac{\pi}{4}\cos\alpha-\sin\frac{\pi}{4}\sin\alpha\frac{7\sqrt2}{10}$

>[!note]
>$\sin(\frac{\pi}{4}-\alpha)=\cos(\frac{\pi}{4}+\alpha)可以从另外一个角度说明吗？$

>[!tip]
>诱导公式
>注意发现$\frac{\pi}{4}+\alpha与\frac{\pi}{4}-\alpha$的和为$\frac{\pi}{2}$
>$\cos(\frac{\pi}{4}+\alpha)=\cos[\frac{\pi}{2}-(\frac{\pi}{4}-\alpha)]=\sin(\frac{\pi}{4}-\alpha)$
