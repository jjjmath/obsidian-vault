>[!success] 利用角度关系
>$已知\theta\in(0,\frac{\pi}{2}),\sin2\theta=\sin(\theta+\frac{2\pi}{3}),则\cos3\theta=()$

>[!解析]
>$\sin2\theta=\sin(\theta+\frac{2\pi}{3})$
>根据诱导公式
>$则2\theta=\theta+\frac{2\pi}{3}+2k\pi或$
>$则2\theta=\pi-(\theta+\frac{2\pi}{3})+2k\pi$
>$\therefore \theta=\frac{2\pi}{3}+2k\pi或\theta=\frac{\pi}{9}+\frac{2k\pi}{3}(k\in Z)$
>$\because \theta\in(0,\frac{\pi}{2}),$
>$\therefore k=0,\theta=\frac{\pi}{9}$
>$\cos3\theta=\cos\frac{\pi}{3}=\frac{1}{2}$
