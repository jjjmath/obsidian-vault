>[!success] 题目
>$已知函数f(x)=\sin(\omega x+\frac{\pi}{3})(\omega>0)在(\frac{\pi}{6},\frac{\pi}{3})上有最小值，无最大值，且f(\frac{\pi}{6})=f(\frac{\pi}{3})$
>$(1)求f(x)的最小正周期$
>$(2),将函数f(x)的图象向右平移\varphi(0<\varphi<\frac{\pi}{6})个单位长度后等到$
>$g(x)的图象，且对满足|f(x_1)-g(x_2)|=2的x_1,x_2有|x_1-x_2|_{\min}=\frac{\pi}{7}$
>$(ⅰ)求\varphi的值$
>$(ⅱ)若对任意的x_1,x_2\in[-\frac{\pi}{7},\frac{\pi}{7}],都有f(x_1)<g(x_2)+m成立$
>$求实数m的取值范围$

>[!help] (1)
>$只有最小值没有最大值,且f(\frac{\pi}{6})=f(\frac{\pi}{3})$
>$所以x=\frac{\pi}{4}时，取得最小值$
>$且\frac{\pi}{3}-\frac{\pi}{6}=\frac{\pi}{6}\le T=\frac{2\pi}{\omega}\Rightarrow 0<\omega\le12$
>$f(\frac{\pi}{4})=\sin(\frac{\pi\omega}{4}+\frac{\pi}{3})=-1\Rightarrow \frac{\pi\omega}{4}+\frac{\pi}{3}=-\frac{\pi}{2}+2k\pi,k\in Z$
>$\omega=-\frac{10}{3}+8k$
>$k只能等于1，\omega=\frac{14}{3}$
>$所以f(x)的最小正周期为\frac{3\pi}{7}$

>[!help] (2)(ⅰ)
>$g(x)=f(x-\varphi)=\sin[\frac{14}{3}(x-\varphi)+\frac{\pi}{3}]$
>$|f(x_1)-g(x_2)|=2$
>$f(x_1)与g(x_2),必然是一个取1，一个取-2$
>$不妨设f(x_1)=1,g(x_2)=-1$
>$则\begin{cases}\frac{14}{3}x_1+\frac{\pi}{3}=\frac{\pi}{2}+2k_1\pi\\\frac{14}{3}(x_2-\varphi)+\frac{\pi}{3}=-\frac{\pi}{2}+2k_2\pi,\end{cases}k_1,k_2\in Z$
>$\begin{cases}x_1=\frac{\pi}{28}+\frac{3k_1\pi}{7}\\x_2=-\frac{5\pi}{28}+\frac{3k_2\pi}{7}+\varphi\end{cases}$
>$x_2-x_1=-\frac{3\pi}{14}+\frac{3\pi}{7}(k_2-k_1)+\varphi$
>$设k_2-k_1=k$
>$x_2-x_1=-\frac{3\pi}{14}+\frac{3\pi}{7}k+\varphi$
>$|x_1-x_2|_{\min}=\frac{\pi}{7},0<\varphi<\frac{\pi}{6}$
>$\therefore k=0\Rightarrow \varphi=\frac{\pi}{14}$

>[!help] (2)(ⅱ)
>$g(x)=\sin\frac{14}{3}x,f(x)=\sin(\frac{14}{3}x+\frac{\pi}{3})$
>$f(x_1)<g(x_2)+m恒成立的条件是f(x_1)_{\max}<[g(x_2)+m]_{\min}$
>$下面求f(x)在[-\frac{\pi}{7},\frac{\pi}{7}]的最大值和g(x)在[-\frac{\pi}{7},\frac{\pi}{7}]的最小值$
>$x\in[-\frac{\pi}{7},\frac{\pi}{7}],\frac{14}{3}x+\frac{\pi}{3}\in[-\frac{\pi}{3},\pi],f(x)_{\max}=1$
>$x\in[-\frac{\pi}{7},\frac{\pi}{7}],\frac{14}{3}x\in[-\frac{2\pi}{3},\frac{2\pi}{3}],g(x)_{\min}=-1$
>$\therefore 1<-1+m\Rightarrow m>2$
