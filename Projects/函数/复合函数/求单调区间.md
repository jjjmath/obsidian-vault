>[!success] 题目3
>$f(x)=\lg\sin x的单调增区间为（）$
>>[!答案]-
>>$先求定义域$
>$\sin x>0\Rightarrow x\in(2k\pi,\pi+2k\pi),k\in Z$
>$\therefore 增区间为(2k\pi,2k\pi+\frac{\pi}{2}),k\in Z$
>
>$\color{red}对比$
>$f(x)=\log_{\frac{1}{2}}\sin x的单调增区间为（）$
>>[!答案]-
>>$先求定义域$
>$\sin x>0\Rightarrow x\in(2k\pi,\pi+2k\pi),k\in Z$
>$\therefore 增区间为(2k\pi+\frac{\pi}{2},2k\pi+\pi),k\in Z$
