>[!success] 三角函数的零点、对称、周期、最值
>$已知函数f(x)=\sin(\omega x+\varphi)(\omega>0,|\varphi|<\frac{\pi}{2},若x_1,x_2是f(x)=\frac{\sqrt2}{2}$
>$连个不同的解，且|x_1-x_2|的最小值为\frac{\pi}{4},下列说法正确的是()$
>- [x] $A.\omega=2$
>- [x] $B.若(\frac{\pi}{3},0)是f(x)的一个对称中心，则\varphi=\frac{\pi}{3}$
>- [ ] $C.当\tan\varphi=2,f(x)在区间(0,\frac{\pi}{6})上的值域为(\frac{2\sqrt5}{5},1]$
>- [x] $D.若f(x)在区间(0,\frac{2\pi}{3})内有最小值，则\frac{\pi}{6}<\varphi<\frac{\pi}{2}$

>[!解析]
>$使得\sin x=\frac{\sqrt2}{2}的最近的两个x值的距离为\frac{\pi}{2}$
>$\therefore |(\omega x_1+\varphi)-(\omega x_2+\varphi)|=\frac{\pi}{2}$
>$\therefore \omega|x_1-x_2|=\frac{\pi}{2}\Rightarrow \omega=2$

>[!continue]
>$(\frac{\pi}{3},0)是f(x)的一个对称中心$
>$则f(\frac{\pi}{3})=0\Rightarrow 2\cdot\frac{\pi}{3}+\varphi=k\pi,k\in Z$
>$\varphi=k\pi-\frac{2\pi}{3}$
>$又|\varphi|<\frac{\pi}{2}，只有k=1成立，\varphi=\frac{\pi}{3}$


>[!continue]
>$0<x<\frac{\pi}{6},\varphi<2x+\varphi<\frac{\pi}{3}+\varphi$
>$\sin\varphi=\frac{2}{\sqrt5},\sin(\frac{\pi}{3}+\varphi)=\frac{2+\sqrt3}{2\sqrt5}<\sin\varphi$
>
>$\therefore 值域为(\frac{2\sqrt5+\sqrt{15}}{10},1]$

>[!continue]
>$0<x<\frac{2\pi}{3}时,\varphi<2x+\varphi<\frac{4\pi}{3}+\varphi$
>$\because y=\sin x 在x=-\frac{\pi}{2}+2k\pi取得最小值$
>$\therefore \begin{cases}|\varphi|<\frac{\pi}{2}\\\varphi<-\frac{\pi}{2}+2k\pi<\varphi+\frac{4\pi}{3}\end{cases},k\in Z$
>$只有k=1时成立\Rightarrow \frac{\pi}{6}<\varphi<\frac{\pi}{2}$
>
