>[!example] **题目1**
>
>$求函数y=\tan(\frac{1}{2}x+\frac{\pi}{3})的定义域，周期，单调区间，值域和对称中心$


>[!note]- 答案
>$定义域\{x|x\neq2k\pi+\frac{\pi}{3},k\in Z\}$
>$周期T=2\pi$
>$单调增区间为(2k\pi-\frac{5\pi}{3},2k\pi+\frac{\pi}{3}),k\in Z$
>$值域为R$
>$对称中心为(k\pi-\frac{2\pi}{3},0),k\in Z$

>[!example] **题目2**

>比较大小
>$(1)\tan(-52^\circ)和\tan(-47^\circ)$
>$(2)\tan(\frac{13\pi}{4})与\tan(\frac{17\pi}{5})$
>$(3)\tan1与\tan4$

>[!tip]- 答案
>$(1)<$
>$(2)设f(x)=\tan x,f(\frac{13\pi}{4})=f(\frac{\pi}{4}),f(\frac{17\pi}{5})=f(\frac{2\pi}{5})$
>$f(x)在(-\frac{\pi}{2},\frac{\pi}{2})递增，-\frac{\pi}{2}<\frac{\pi}{4}<\frac{2\pi}{5}<\frac{\pi}{2},\therefore \tan(\frac{13\pi}{4})<\tan(\frac{17\pi}{5})$
>$(3)\tan4=\tan(4-\pi),0<4-\pi<1<\frac{\pi}{2},\therefore \tan1>\tan4$
