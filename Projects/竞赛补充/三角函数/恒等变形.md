>[!success] 题目1
>$求值:\tan15^\circ+2\sqrt2\sin15^\circ$

>[!解析]-
>$\tan15^\circ+2\sqrt2\sin15^\circ=\frac{\sin15^\circ}{\cos15^\circ}+2\sqrt2\sin15^\circ$
>$=\frac{\sin15^\circ+\sqrt2\sin30^\circ}{\cos15^\circ}$
>$=\frac{\sin15^\circ+\sqrt2\sin(45^\circ-15^\circ)}{\cos15^\circ}$
>$=1$

>[!success] 题目2
>$若\tan x\tan y=2,\sin x\sin y=\frac{1}{3},求x-y$

>[!解析]
>$由已知可得\frac{\sin x\sin y}{\cos x\cos y}=2\Rightarrow \sin x\sin y=2\cos x\cos y$
>$\therefore \cos x\cos y=\frac{1}{6}$
>$\cos(x-y)=\frac{1}{2}$
>$x-y=2k\pi\pm\frac{\pi}{3},k\in Z$
